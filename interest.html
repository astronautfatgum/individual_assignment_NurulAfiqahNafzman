Interests

History

History is an interest of mine since forever! I'm a huge geek about the civilisation, the history of individuals and places. My favourite history of individuals includes Hang Tuah, Joan of Arc, Mahsuri, the Sultan of Melaka, Tun Perak and Vincent Van Gogh. As per civilisation, I love the history of Russia, Asia and Roman and Egyptian! For places, I love Melaka, Athens and Langkawi!

Music

As for music, I love various of genres. Mainly making the artists my centre attention rather than the genre. I have been a huge fan of BTS since 2016! Liking their music has opened so many doors for me to discovers many talented artists such as Weezer, Tyler The Creator, MF DOOM, Clairo, Hatsune Miku (Vocaloid overall), Twice, and Frank Ocean also many more! Even before I know BTS, I have been such a huge fan of music altogether. I remember being obsessed with the music released by Yuna, Stacy, Najwa Latiff and bunch of songs from my dad's collection.

TV Shows and movies I love are Coraline, Smiling Friends, Chiikawa, The Good Place, Zombi Kampung Pisang.
